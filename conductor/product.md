# Product Guide

## Initial Concept
An AI-driven price comparison ecosystem using Laravel and pgvector to scrape and match products across retailers, allowing users to instantly find the lowest prices via image or text search.

## Target Users
Price-conscious shoppers looking for the best deals.

## Core Features
The core features include price comparison (not necessarily real-time), image-based product search, text-based product search with advanced filtering, price history tracking and alert notifications, and integration with various e-commerce platforms.

## Unique Selling Proposition
Superior accuracy and breadth of product matching through advanced AI and `pgvector` technology.
