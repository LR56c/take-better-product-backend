# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style
The product should communicate with its users in a friendly and encouraging tone, using conversational language with positive and supportive phrasing.

## Brand Messaging
The core brand message is "Empowerment and Savings," focusing on empowering users to make informed purchasing decisions and save money effortlessly.

## Visual Identity
The visual identity should convey a modern and clean aesthetic, emphasizing sleek, minimalist design with clear typography and intuitive layouts.
